ZScheduler
----------

Zope server based scheduling for a variety of back-ends - including crontab.

Use the ZScheduleEvent class to control scheduling of content and use it's Plone skins
for UI.

You are encouraged to offer feedback through http://www.last-bastion.net/forge/zscheduler.

For full documentation, you are recommended to use pydoc, or even our
http://www.last-bastion.net/forge/zpydoc Zope plug-in.
